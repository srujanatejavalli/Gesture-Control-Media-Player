Privacy & Data Policy

We respect your privacy. Gesture Player processes input from your device locally; camera video frames are not uploaded to our servers. We collect minimal telemetry such as anonymized usage events and optional feedback messages you submit. We retain this data only as needed to improve the product and to respond to support requests.

Data Retention

Feedback and support messages are retained until resolved. You can request removal of personal data by contacting support@gesture.com.
