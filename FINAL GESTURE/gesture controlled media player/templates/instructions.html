Important Tips for Best Results

Hand Position

Keep your hand 1–2 feet from camera

Show your palm clearly to the camera

Hold gestures for 1–2 seconds

Gesture Accuracy

Spread fingers apart clearly

Avoid overlapping fingers

Keep hand steady while gesturing

Performance

Close other camera applications

Allow camera permissions when prompted

Browser Suggestion

Use Chrome or Firefox for best results
