Need Help?

Common Issues

  • Camera not working: Check browser permissions
  • Gestures not detected: Improve lighting, show palm clearly
  • Video not loading: Ensure it's a valid YouTube URL
  • Slow response: Hold gesture for 1-2 seconds

Tips

  • Keep hand 1–2 feet from camera
  • Face a light source for best detection
  • Spread fingers clearly apart
  • Use keyboard shortcuts as backup
