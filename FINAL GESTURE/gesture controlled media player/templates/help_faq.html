Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using the Gesture Controlled Media Player.
Why are my gestures not being recognized?

Common causes include poor lighting, camera angle, or slow network. Try repositioning and ensure gestures are clearly visible to the camera.

Is my data private?

We do not upload or store video streams; only telemetry and feedback may be saved. See our Privacy & Data Policy for details.

Which browsers are supported?

Chrome and Edge are recommended. Mobile support is limited and depends on camera permission APIs.

How do I submit a bug?

Use the Contact Support or Feedback page to send screenshots and details of the behavior. We appreciate reproducible steps.
